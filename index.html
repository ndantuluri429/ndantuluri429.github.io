Bio

Hi! I am a 2nd year MMSc Biomedical Informatics candidate at Harvard Medical School. My research focuses on using AI/ML to support clinical decision-making by improving the interpretability and fairness of predictive models deployed in healthcare settings. My work spans multiple clinical domains, with a particular interest in maternal-fetal health.
